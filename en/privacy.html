Privacy policy

Prompt Anatomy – minimal app. A brief note about your data.

Do we collect your data?

No. We do not collect any personal data at this time. There is no form, email collection, or sending to servers.

What happens on your device?

Only your browser’s localStorage: we save which prompts you marked as “Done”. Data stays on your device only.

If we add a form later

If we enable a contact form or similar, this policy will be updated – we will clearly state what we collect and how we use it.
